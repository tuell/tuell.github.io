The Rules

Previous
Start game

Start the game by clicking on the "New game"-button on the top right.

Choose a category

You are shown your own card and the country of the computer's card. Choose a category that you think is worse in your country by clicking on the number.

See result

You are shown the result of your choice: The player who wins gets one point. Start the next turn with the button in the top right corner.

See summary

After the last card is played, you can see a summary of each turn by clicking on the button.

Start new game

If you want to play again, you can click the button in the top right corner.
