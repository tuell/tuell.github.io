The Rules

"Inequality Top Flops" lets the user play against a computer.

Start the game

When you press the "New Game"-Button, you and the computer are dealt ten random cards each. The first of your cards is shown to you.

Make your move

Choose one of the categories by clicking on its value.

See the result

The computer card is shown and you can see who won.

  • Carbon footprint: Higher value wins
  • Price of 1 liter of milk: Higher price wins
  • Freedom of Press Rank: Lower rank wins
  • Percentage of people with internet access: Lower percentage wins
  • Life expectancy: Lower number wins
  • Year in which women got equal voting rights: Later year wins

Goal

The goal is to collect more points than the computer. When all cards are played, you can see who won by clicking on the "Summary"-Button.
