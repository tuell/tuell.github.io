The Game

"Inequality Top Flops" is a card game about different categories of inequality. It is played like "Super Trumps" (aka "Quartett" in German): The cards show different categories of ineqalities for a specific country. Players compete by choosing categories and comparing values. The worst value wins.

See the rules

The Inequalities

Each card lists the statistics for one country in seven categories: Carbon emissions per person, the price of one liter of milk, the Freedom of Press Ranking according to Reporters Without Borders, the percentage of people with internet access, the life expectancy and the year in which women got equal voting rights.

More info about the categories

The Idea

This game is the result of a hackathon of Deutsche Welle and BBC in June 2018. Based on the premise of visualising inequalities in the world with a DDJ-approach, our team developped the idea of a card game that let's you compare inequalities between different countries.
